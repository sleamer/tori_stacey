Work Force Screenings (WFS) is a series of documentary films focusing on labor struggles of the past that will inform organizing of today. WFS uses film to connect all those who fight for safer workplaces, humane working conditions, and a more lively and cohesive political organ. Hosted weekly at RECESS. Co-presenters and facilitators have included Marc Moscato (Know Your City), Right to Survive, Anne LaFleur, Hart Noecker, and Paige Shell-Spurling.
